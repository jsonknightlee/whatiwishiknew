Wiwik, An Experiential Knowledge and Skill Transfer Community
Have you ever wondered what it would be like to learn a new skill in half the time it would normally take?


Not so long ago if you wanted to learn something new you had to go to a library or bookshop. The transfer of knowledge from everything from skydiving to carpentry was mostly book-based. Another option was to ask family and friends for tips, tricks and advise. You could have also join a club or just go to a shop where they sell the stuff and ask around there to find out more about what you wanted to do.

We used to spend most of our time just finding a resource, person or place where we could learn more to get started.

Today with search engines and the open source sharing platforms you can find out just about anything about everything.
So what if today you took on a skill that's more geared towards you making a shift in your career or amassing a passive income?
Your attempts will not just be those of the avid hobbyist but needs to be worth your time and effort it will also need to cost you a fraction of an Ivy league education. Enter WIWIK - A community where people can share and educate people with skills and resources from their own experiences. WIWIK which stands for What I Wish I Knew(before I built my own skate ramp and it broke my fall when it broke...). Users can post their processes and efforts to help others avoid the pitfalls and frustrations that should not necessarily be a part of the process. Giving NOOBS a heading and equiping them with the tools to acquire skills.

We encourage users to post WIWIKS that include humor and wit and keep it lighthearted as far as possible, because most of us want to have fun learning not dread any of it, and thus you will be rewarded with recommendations from other users. Furthermore feel free to include resources and links to your own websites, blogs, vlogs or training centers. Because when you think back just like us can recount a "What I Wish I Knew Before I Started" element from your journey to success and stardom. Give the gift of knowledge and empower others and we will empower you!

You can post anything as long as the content is filled with knowledge and is insightful to a particular demographic who would find the information useful.

Philosophy


The concept behind our ideology can be discovered in Tibetan Buddhism where a chosen child is to replace the Dalai Lama who had passed on. The Dalai Lama at his end is said to practice active dying in order to release his consciousness into the world to be reincarnated into another being through whom the Dalai Lama is reincarnated. This child is said to be the reborn Dalai Lama and is raised for high priesthood. All the wisdom and knowlegde is already with the chosen child because the child is the Dalai Lama. Therefore the ascensions to greater levels and higher spiritually will just be a natural process.

In the same way as the chosen child possesses the wisdom and skills and wisdom from the previous Dalai Lama. We believe that every human has inborn wisdom, knowledge and potential. The choice to learn something new and challenging is to be an expression of these inborn "gifts" and must be encouraged to further the human race and help people free themselves to dare to do what they dream.

So join us on this journey towards knowledge learning and having fun doing it!
